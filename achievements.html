Achievements

Searching for descriptions and help on how to get
those last (or maybe secret) achievements?
Check the list below for the ones you're missing,
we've added some guides to help you on your way!

Name Description

Reparing the Weave

Stabilise Gale's Netherese orb. (Secret)

Fetch Quest

Play fetch with Scratch - the best boy in the realms.

Homebrewer

Create three unique alchemical solutions in a single playthrough - bottoms up!

Kill Two Birds With One Gnome

Use one enemy as an improvised weapon against another.

No Penny Required

Successfully use Detect Thoughts to pry into someone's thoughts.

Non-Invasive Procedure

Kill the Surgeon before he performs surgery on you.

Outsourcing

Recruit a hireling. You can befriend them or use them as cannon fodder. We won't Judge.

Leave No One Behind

Save every tiefling refugee you can throughout the game in a single playthrough. (Secret)

Hot Date

Go on a date with Karlach - now that's playing with fire.

Loophole

Break Wyll's Pact with Mizora. (Secret)

First Blood

Kill Orin while her cultists are performing their ritual chant. (Secret)

Devil's in the Details

Defeat Commander Zhalk in the Nautiloid.

Dig for Victory

Dig up five buried chests in a single playthrough - treasure!

Interfectorem Draconis

Kill the Red Dragon in the Upper City. (Secret)

Penny Pincher

Defeat the Toll Collector without her using gold against you - excellent budgeting.

Mind Blown

Romance the Emperor. (Secret)

Pest Control

Kill the Spider Matriarch before her eggs hatch - why do they have so many legs? (Secret)

Forged in Blood and Fire

Craft an item in the Adamantine Forge. (Secret)

Just a Nibble

Let Astarion bite you at night - ouch. (Secret)

No Free Lunches

Defeat the Apostle of Myrkul before it consumes any Necromites. (Secret)

Embrace Your Urge

Become Bhaal's ultimate weapon - become his Slayer. (Secret)
